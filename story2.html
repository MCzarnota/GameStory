Chapter 2:Inside the darkness

The air around you is warm and moist.

The smell of rotting flesh assails your nostrils. You know that something lurk inside, something evil.

You descend the battered stairway of wood that leads deep further into the mansion, your legs already weary from the forced march. You cannot pause for rest; too great is your burden. If only you can find the culprit of those all murders - then you will be able to stop the townpeople from worrying. Once that is accomplished, and only then, will you rest.

Your torches flicker, wavering ever so slightly in the dry breeze that comes from within the corridor. Ahead of you, at the very edge of the dancing lights, the passageway halts and branches off in two different locations.

You decide to go left while your tell your companion to go right. After a short while you noticed a door at the end of this path. Suprisingly your companion is already waiting there. Heh so it led to the same direction you think. The door is made of pure golden like wood, detailed. It seems to be countless years old.

The wind here is stronger, your torches barely resisting the random gusts, and some foul and unusual odor floods your nostrils. Whatever call this place its home must be evil... or at the very least, without a nose.

As you shield your torches from the brutal wind, and your noses from the terrible scent, you decide to bash the door.

You muttered under your nose:

Shor, Mara, Dibella, Kynareth, Akatosh! Divines, please help me!" I hope this smell will not kill me!

You kick the door so hard that it explodes.

Shower of splinters barrages the floor, announcing your presence.

Your head moves left then right in rapid order, your keen mind taking in the details of darkened chamber. Out of the corner of your eye you see a rotted skeleton laying in the corner. It's so old and so badly mauled, the bones shattered in numerous pieces and yellow with age, that it is impossible to identfy it's race.

Human most likely, you think, based on its size and equipment. You decide to move on.. suddenly a powerful wind rushes from the corridor that lies before you. You try to shield your torches but are too slow.

The wind extinguishes all of your torches, though in the split second before the darkness envelops your group you can't help but see a tall monstrous silhouette.. IT'S AN ORG!

You hear him in the darkness , moving towards you...
